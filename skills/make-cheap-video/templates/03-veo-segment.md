# Veo Lite Segment Prompt

Write this as a standalone prompt. Do not assume the model saw a previous
segment. No subtitles, no captions, no logos, no readable text.

```
<SEGMENT A or SEGMENT B>. 8-second cinematic video, 16:9, 720p.

STYLE: <medium and look, e.g. cyberpunk anime, hand-drawn ink lines, cel shading, neon rain, high contrast, no photorealism>.

CHARACTERS: <protagonist exact appearance and emotional posture>. <opposing force exact appearance and scale>.

LOCATION: <environment and spatial anchors>.

CAMERA AND BEATS:
0-2s: <beat one with camera direction>.
2-5s: <beat two with camera direction>.
5-8s: <beat three with camera direction and end frame>.

AUDIO: <diegetic sound effects and ambience only; mention any synced impact or silence>.

NEGATIVE: no subtitles, no captions, no text, no logos, no extra characters, no scene reset, no comedy unless the narrative asks for it.
```
